## About

FollowDiff is a free tool that generates a list of who doesn't follow back and who you don't follow back on Instagram, made in TypeScript with [Next.js](https://nextjs.org/), [RadixUI](https://www.radix-ui.com/) and [JSZip](https://stuk.github.io/jszip/).

- Visit the [site](https://follow-diff.vercel.app/) to see how it works
- Contributions are welcome

## Getting Started

1. Head to Instagram's **Account Center** > **Your information and permissions** > **Export your information**
2. Press **Create export** > **Export to device**
3. Click on **Customize information** and unselect everything except **Followers and following**, then press **Save**
4. Click on **Format** and select **JSON**, then press **Save**. Be sure to also select **All time** for **Date range** and then press **Save**. Once done, press **Start export**.
5. Your information will take some time to download. Instagram will notify you once your information is ready via your email.
6. Once your information is ready, download your ZIP file then upload it to this site via the **Upload** button.
